HoS Demo Index - Swirling Water

This is a collection of demos that show the ability to use WebGL and WebGPU to create a swirling water effect on mobile devices.

Users will be prompted to approve the use of their accelerometer.

Demos:

  • Example 1 - Accelerometer WEBGL GPU
  • Example 2 - WebGPU Compute Fluid Particles
  • Example 3 - WebGPU Compute Water
